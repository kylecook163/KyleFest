Privacy Policy

This page is written to work both as your public website privacy section and as the policy users can access from the App Store.

Your party. Your people. Your privacy.

KyleFest is a local-first social app designed for use between friends and groups in shared environments. Most activity is built around party sessions and nearby interactions rather than a public feed.

Last updated: March 2026 Intended for 18+ No tracking

1. Information we collect

  • Account information such as email address, display name, and optional profile photo
  • User content such as messages, direct messages, photos, videos, polls, bets, and game activity
  • App data such as user identifiers, party activity, achievements, stats, and in-app progress

2. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To enable messaging, shared media, and party features
  • To display relevant activity inside your party session
  • To maintain app functionality and improve reliability

3. Local networking and session sharing

  • Most interactions are intended to happen directly between devices in the same party experience
  • Shared content is meant to be visible only to users participating in that party
  • KyleFest is not intended to function as a public social network or open global feed

4. Data storage

  • Some account-related data may be stored using service providers required to operate the app
  • Some information may also be stored locally on your device
  • Party content may be exchanged locally and may not be permanently stored on external servers

5. Data sharing

  • We do not sell personal information
  • Data may be shared with other users only as needed for the party experience
  • Data may also be shared with service providers that support core app functions such as authentication

6. User controls

  • Users can update profile information
  • Users can leave parties at any time
  • Users can block or hide other users locally within the app experience

7. Children’s privacy

KyleFest is intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information used by the app, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of KyleFest after changes means you accept the revised policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or the app can be sent to support@kylefest.app.

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about what KyleFest is, how it works, and what kind of experience it is built for.

What is KyleFest?

KyleFest is a local-first party app that combines chat, shared media, games, polls, party tools, and quick navigation into one experience for groups hanging out together.

Is KyleFest a public social network?

No. It is designed around private party sessions and group experiences rather than public feeds or open discovery.

What can you do in the app?

You can message the group, send DMs, use the camera, view shared photos and videos, track games, run polls, manage party activity, and use the Home dashboard to keep up with everything.

Who is the app for?

KyleFest is built for friend groups, parties, cabins, weekends away, events, and other local group hangouts where people want one shared app experience.

Will an Android version come out?

No — KyleFest is built for iOS. Maybe one day… but right now we’re keeping it in the Apple ecosystem.

Why is it local-first?

Because the app is meant to feel immediate and connected to the people around you right now, not to a giant public network of strangers.
