Privacy

Your party. Your people. Your privacy.

This page works as your public website privacy section and as the policy users can access from the App Store.

The short version.

KyleFest doesn't sell your data, doesn't show ads, and doesn't share your information with advertisers. Most of your data stays on your device. The only thing we keep on our servers is your account profile, if you choose to create one. Party chats, games, photos, and other activity stay between you and your party — they aren't saved to any server after the party ends. You can delete your account anytime in Settings.

Last updated: April 2026 Intended for 18+ No tracking

1. What we collect

  • Account info: if you create an account, we store your email address, display name, and password. If you use Sign in with Apple, we receive an identity token from Apple — we never see your Apple ID password.
  • Profile: your display name and profile photo are saved on your device. If you have an account, your display name also syncs so you can use it across devices.
  • Game stats: wins, losses, ratings, CookPoints, and achievements are saved on your device.
  • Moderation preferences: if you mute, hide, or block someone, that's saved on your device only.

2. Party & social data

  • When you join a party, your display name, profile photo, and game activity are shared with the other party members over your local Wi-Fi network.
  • Chat messages, game sessions, bets, polls, photos, and videos are sent in real time between devices in the party. This information isn't saved to any server — it stays in the app while the party is going and is cleared when you leave.
  • Direct messages between party members are end-to-end encrypted, so only the sender and receiver can read them.
  • Other party members can see your display name, photo, game activity, and chat messages while you're in the same party. If you mute, hide, or block someone, only you see the effect — they aren't notified.

3. How we use your data

  • To identify you in parties and sync across your devices
  • To track achievements, leaderboards, and ratings
  • To apply your mute, hide, and block choices on your device
  • We don't sell your data, show ads, or share your information with advertisers

4. Where your data is stored

  • On your device: profile, photo, preferences, game stats, and moderation choices
  • On our servers: if you create an account, your email, display name, and login credentials are stored with Supabase, our authentication provider
  • iCloud: some account info and game stats may sync through your personal iCloud account so they're available on your other devices
  • Not stored anywhere long-term: chat, photos, videos, game state, and party activity. These stay in the app during a party and are cleared when you leave

5. Photos, videos & camera

  • The app uses your camera to take photos and videos to share with your party. We only access the camera when you choose to use it.
  • If you pick a photo from your library to share, only the photos you select are sent. We don't browse your full library.
  • You can choose to save photos or videos from the party back to your photo library.
  • Photos and videos shared in a party are sent directly between devices and aren't uploaded to any server.

6. Face recognition (on your device)

  • To help tag party members in shared photos, the app uses Apple's on-device face recognition to suggest who appears in each photo.
  • All face recognition happens on your device. No face data, photos, or recognition results ever leave your device or get sent to any server.
  • You can opt out of face suggestions in Settings.

7. Voice calls & microphone

  • The microphone is used for voice calls with party members and for recording videos. We only access it when you choose to use those features.
  • Voice calls happen directly between devices on your local network. They aren't routed through any server, and we don't record or store them.

8. Location & nearby devices

  • Apple requires location permission for apps that use the local network to find nearby devices. We use this only to discover other party members on your Wi-Fi — we don't track or store your location.
  • On supported devices, the app may use Ultra Wideband to estimate how close other party members are. This stays on your device and isn't sent to any server.

9. Apple Watch

If you use the KyleFest Apple Watch app, your phone and watch share party info with each other directly. Nothing extra is sent to any server.

10. Notifications

  • The app uses Apple's push notification service to send you alerts about parties, messages, and games. Apple gives the app a notification token for your device — we use this only to deliver KyleFest notifications.
  • You can turn off notifications anytime in your device settings or in the app.

11. Music

  • If you add songs to a shared party playlist, the app uses Apple Music to search for and play tracks. An Apple Music subscription is required to play full songs from the catalog.
  • Your music listening is handled by Apple Music — we don't store your listening history or share it.

12. Game Center

  • The app uses Apple Game Center to track achievements. If you sign in to Game Center, your achievements are reported to Apple under your Game Center account.
  • Game Center is run by Apple. You can manage it in your device settings.

13. Account deletion

  • You can delete your account anytime from Settings → Data & Account → Delete Account. This signs you out and removes your account.
  • Your local data on this device is cleared right away.
  • Your account info on our servers is removed as part of the deletion request. If the server is temporarily unavailable, your account is queued for removal.

14. Data shared with other users

  • While you're in a party, other members can see your display name, profile photo, game activity, chat messages, bets, poll votes, and any media you share.
  • Your email address is never shared with other users.
  • When you mute, hide, or block someone, the other person is not notified.

15. Third-party services

  • Supabase: we use Supabase to handle account sign-up and login.
  • Apple: we use Apple services for Sign in with Apple, iCloud sync, push notifications, Apple Music, and Game Center.
  • We don't use any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

16. Data security

  • Connections between devices in a party are encrypted, so others on the network can't read your party traffic.
  • Direct messages between party members are end-to-end encrypted — only you and the person you're messaging can read them. We have no way to access them.
  • Your password is securely hashed by our authentication provider. We never see or store your plain-text password.

17. Data retention

  • Party activity (chat, games, bets, photos, videos) is cleared when you leave the party or close the app.
  • Profile, preferences, and game stats stay on your device until you delete your account or uninstall the app.
  • Account info on our servers is removed within 30 days of an account deletion request.

18. Data export

You can export your account data anytime from Settings → Data & Account → Export Data. You'll get a file with your profile, stats, and preferences. Party activity isn't included because it isn't stored after the party ends.

19. No tracking or analytics

  • KyleFest doesn't use any analytics, tracking, or behavioral monitoring tools.
  • We don't track your behavior, build advertising profiles, or sell your data.

20. Children

KyleFest is intended for adults of legal drinking age. The app is not designed for and should not be used by children.

21. Your rights

  • You can access, export, and delete your data anytime in Settings.
  • If you live in the EU, UK, or California, you have additional rights under your local privacy laws. Email us at KyleFestApp@gmail.com to make a request.

22. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy or your data? Email us at KyleFestApp@gmail.com.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Quick answers to the most common questions about KyleFest.

What is KyleFest?

KyleFest is a local-first party app that combines chat, shared media, games, polls, party tools, and quick navigation into one experience for groups hanging out together.

Is KyleFest a public social network?

No. It's designed around private party sessions and group experiences rather than public feeds or open discovery.

What can you do in the app?

You can message the group, send DMs, make voice calls, use the camera, view shared photos and videos, track games and tournaments, play casino-style games for fun, run polls, build a shared Apple Music playlist, and use the Home dashboard to keep up with everything.

Is there an Apple Watch app?

Yes — KyleFest comes with a companion Apple Watch app so you can stay connected to the party from your wrist when your phone is in your pocket.

Who is the app for?

KyleFest is built for friend groups, parties, cabins, weekends away, events, and other local group hangouts where people want one shared app experience.

Will an Android version come out?

No — KyleFest is built for iOS. Maybe one day… but right now we're keeping it in the Apple ecosystem.

Why is it local-first?

Because the app is meant to feel immediate and connected to the people around you right now, not to a giant public network of strangers.
