KyleFest Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

KyleFest (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is a locally-based social app designed for use between friends and groups in shared environments. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information
Email address, display name, and optional profile photo.

User Content
Messages, photos, videos, and game activity within parties.

App Data
User identifiers, session activity, and in-app progress.

2. How We Use Your Information

To provide core app functionality including accounts, messaging, party interactions, and improving user experience.

3. Local Networking

Most interactions occur directly between devices using local connections. Data is only visible to users within the same party.

4. Data Storage

Account data may be stored using secure third-party services. Some data is stored locally on your device.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Data is only shared with other users in your party or required service providers.

6. User Controls

Users can update profiles, leave parties, and block or hide others.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for users 18+.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information.

9. Changes

We may update this policy over time.

10. Contact

Email: support@kylefest.app
