KyleFest · Privacy Policy

Your party. Your people. Your privacy.

KyleFest is built for local, in-person experiences. Most activity happens directly between devices in the same party session, not through a public social network.

Last updated: March 2026
Intended for 18+
Local-first experience

1. Information We Collect

KyleFest may collect the following information to make the app work as intended:

  • Account information: email address, display name, and optional profile photo.
  • User content: chat messages, direct messages, photos, videos, polls, bets, and game activity shared within a party.
  • App data: user identifiers, achievements, stats, party/session activity, and in-app progress.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable messaging, party features, and local group interactions
  • Display shared media and game activity to party members
  • Maintain app features, preferences, and user progress
  • Improve reliability and core app functionality

3. Local Networking and Data Sharing

KyleFest is designed primarily around local connections, such as Wi-Fi or nearby device communication.

  • Most party interactions happen directly between devices in the same session.
  • Shared content is intended to be visible only to users participating in that party.
  • KyleFest does not operate as a public feed or open social platform.

4. Data Storage

  • Some account-related data, such as authentication and profile details, may be stored using service providers needed to operate the app.
  • Some information is stored locally on your device.
  • Party content may be exchanged locally and may not be permanently stored on external servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

  • Data may be shared with other users only as needed for the party session and app functionality.
  • Data may also be shared with service providers that help run core features such as account services.

6. User Controls

  • Update your profile information
  • Leave parties at any time
  • Block or hide other users locally within the app experience

7. Children’s Privacy

KyleFest is intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information used by the app. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of KyleFest after updates means you accept the revised policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@kylefest.app.

Quick Summary

KyleFest is local-first. Most party content stays within the party experience and is shared with the people in that session.

What we collect

  • Email and profile info
  • Messages and shared media
  • Party stats and app activity

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal data
  • We do not run a public global feed
  • We do not use your data for tracking
